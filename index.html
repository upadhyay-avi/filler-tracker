Why I built this.

While working at an Advertising Company, a traditional ad agency handling newspaper publications, I witnessed a massive workflow bottleneck. The team was manually generating dozens of daily Release Orders using CorelDraw and tracking them in physical paper registers.

This manual process led to frequent errors, duplicated RO numbers, and significant delays. Furthermore, late-night ad placements required someone to physically boot up a PC to draft the documents—a huge waste of time in the modern digital age.

I searched for an online solution, but the existing SaaS products were bloated and far too expensive for a small business. They didn't want another recurring monthly bill.

So, I built this custom, serverless web application for them from scratch. Now, their team can instantly generate, correct, and track pixel-perfect release orders from anywhere, even directly from their mobile phones.

I understand the burden of expensive software subscriptions on small businesses. That's why I am offering to set up and customize this exact system for your business for a minimal, one-time flat fee.
